What is Themes Bootstrap?

Themes Bootstrap is a collection of free to download Bootstrap themes and templates. Do I have to credit Themes Bootstrap? No, but that sure is a nice thing to do! Our themes are open source and you can use them for any purpose, even commercially. Find out more about Bootstrap on its official website.
